About Us

"Riyadh Guide" is a website that provides visitors with a comprehensive directory of interesting places in Riyadh. Our website features include detailed descriptions of each place, as well as the ability to add your ratings and reviews for the places you have visited.

The main benefit of the website is to serve as a one-stop platform for individuals who are planning to explore Riyadh. It saves visitors time and effort by providing all necessary information in one place, allowing them to easily discover and learn about the city's attractions.
